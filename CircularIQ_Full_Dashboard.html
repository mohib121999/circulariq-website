0.0/10
Blocked
NaCl 150 g/L (limit: 10 g/L) — disables all biological pathways. Stream isolated.
Why this gate exists: Salt concentrations above 10 g/L inhibit microbial communities required for algae cultivation, anaerobic digestion, and struvite precipitation. High NaCl also disrupts plant uptake in irrigation scenarios, violating EU 2020/741 Class A/B limits.

Phase 3 option: Electrodialysis or evaporation-crystallisation could recover food-grade NaCl at ~€80–150/t, but requires separate pilot investment.
